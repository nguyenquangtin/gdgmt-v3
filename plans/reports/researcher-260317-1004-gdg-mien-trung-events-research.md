# GDG Mien Trung Events Research Report

**Date:** March 17, 2026
**Scope:** All past and current events organized by GDG Mien Trung (Da Nang, Vietnam)
**Research Period:** October 2015 – Present

---

## Executive Summary

GDG Mien Trung (Google Developer Group for Central Vietnam) was founded in October 2015 by Nguyễn Thị Phương Nhi. The community has organized 20+ major events over the past decade, with a primary focus on three signature event series: DevFest (annual hackathon), Google I/O Extended (annual conference), and WomenTechmakers (professional development for women in tech). Recent additions include Build With AI (2025 onwards).

## Event Series Summary

### DevFest (Annual Hackathon)
- **Started:** 2016
- **Format Evolution:** 48-hour hackathon (2016-2021) → Conference format (2022 onwards)
- **Themes:** Smart cities, environmental protection, Fintech/Blockchain, AI/E-commerce, responsible AI
- **Typical Attendance:** 20-100+ teams, 400+ participants
- **Status:** Active, annual event

### Google I/O Extended (Annual Conference)
- **Started:** 2016
- **Duration:** Typically July/June dates
- **Attendance Growth:** 600+ (2016) → 1,600+ (2023) → 700+ (2024-2025)
- **Format:** Conference with talks, workshops, networking, codelabs
- **Signature Features:** Expert speakers (domestic + international), free registration, stamp collection games
- **Iterations in Da Nang:** 9 confirmed (2016-2025)
- **Status:** Active, flagship annual event

### WomenTechmakers (Professional Development)
- **Started:** 2016
- **Focus:** Visibility, community, professional development for women in tech
- **Known Events:** Women TechMaker 2016 (~200 attendees), WomenTechmaker GDG MienTrung 2023
- **Attendance:** ~300+ women from tech companies, universities, startups
- **Status:** Ongoing initiative

### Build With AI (Emerging Series)
- **Started:** 2024/2025
- **Focus:** Generative AI, Gemini, practical AI applications
- **Format:** Hands-on workshops and codelabs
- **Status:** New, growing series

### Other Recurring Elements
- **QuánQuân GCP:** Google Cloud learning challenge support sessions (2025+)
- **TechSips:** Speaker series with technical deep dives (2024+)

## Key Insights

1. **Community Growth:** GDG Mien Trung has demonstrated consistent growth from 250 participants (2015) to 700-1,600+ attendees per major event by 2023-2025

2. **Pioneer Status:** First GDG chapter in Vietnam to organize DevFest in 48-hour hackathon format (2016)

3. **Diverse Programming:** Community now offers 4+ major event series plus training programs, reflecting evolution from single-focus community to comprehensive tech hub

4. **International Recognition:** Events attract speakers and participants from US, Singapore, and across Vietnam; Google I/O Extended became flagship regional event

5. **Women in Tech Focus:** Early adoption of WomenTechmakers program (2016) shows sustained gender diversity commitment

6. **AI Transition:** Observable shift toward AI-focused content (2023 hackathon theme, Build With AI series launch 2025) reflecting industry trends

7. **Location Stability:** All events consistently held in Da Nang area; primary venue partnerships with Novotel, Swinburne University Vietnam, Danang Semiconductor & AI Center
